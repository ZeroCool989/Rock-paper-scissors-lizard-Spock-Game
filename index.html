Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock - Rules
Choices: Players can choose from:

  • Rock
  • Paper
  • Scissors
  • Lizard
  • Spock
Winning Conditions:
  • Rock crushes Scissors and Lizard.
  • Scissors cuts Paper and decapitates Lizard.
  • Paper covers Rock and disproves Spock.
  • Lizard eats Paper and poisons Spock.
  • Spock smashes Scissors and vaporizes Rock.
How to Play: The player chooses one of the options, while the computer randomly selects one. Both choices are revealed simultaneously. The winner is determined based on the winning conditions. If both choices are the same, it results in a tie.
Note: This version of the game involves a single player competing against a computer, adding an element of unpredictability to each round.
